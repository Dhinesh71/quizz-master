# QuizMaster User Guide

Welcome to **QuizMaster**, a secure and easy-to-use platform for creating and taking online quizzes with built-in anti-cheating measures.

This document provides step-by-step instructions for **Quiz Creators** (Teachers/Admins) and **Quiz Takers** (Students/Participants).

### 1. Account Setup
- **Sign Up/Login**: Create an account or log in with your email and password to access the dashboard.

### 2. Creating a Quiz
1.  Navigate to the **Dashboard** or click **"Create Quiz"** in the navigation bar.
2.  **Quiz Details**:
    -   **Title**: Enter a clear, descriptive title (e.g., "History Midterm").
    -   **Description**: Add instructions or context (optional).
3.  **Adding Questions**:
    -   Type your question in the "Question Text" box.
    -   Add answer options (minimum 2, maximum 5).
    -   **Select the Correct Answer**: Click the radio button next to the correct option.
    -   Use the **+ Add Question** button to add more questions.
    -   Use the **Up/Down Arrows** to reorder questions.
    -   Use the **Trash Icon** to delete a question.
4.  **Finish**: Click **"Create Quiz"** to save.

### 3. Sharing & Managing Quizzes
-   **Dashboard View**: You will see a list of all your created quizzes.
-   **Link Sharing**:
    -   Click the **Share** button on a quiz card.
    -   **Copy Link**: Send this unique link to your students via email, chat, or LMS.
-   **Settings**:
    -   **Active Status**: Toggle the quiz "Active" or "Inactive". If inactive, students cannot access it.
    -   **Time Limits**: Set a "Valid From" and "Valid Until" date/time to automatically open and close the quiz window.

### 4. Viewing Results
-   Click on a quiz card to view detailed analytics.
-   **Metrics**: See Average Score, Total Responses, and Pass/Fail percentages.
-   **Student List**: View individual student scores, submission times, and contact details.
-   **Export**: Click **"Export CSV"** to download the results for Excel or Google Sheets.

### 1. Requirements
-   **Device**: A laptop, desktop, or tablet is recommended. Mobile phones work but are strictly monitored.
-   **Browser**: Use a modern browser like Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox.
-   **Connection**: Ensure a stable internet connection.

### 2. Starting the Quiz
1.  Open the link provided by your instructor.
2.  **Registration**:
    -   You will see a "Student Information" form.
    -   Enter your **Full Name**, **Email**, **Phone Number**, and **Register Number**.
    -   **Note**: You can only attempt the quiz **ONCE** with your email/register number. Double-check your details!
3.  **Anti-Cheating Warning**:
    -   Read the warnings carefully. This quiz is monitored.
    -   **3 Violations = Automatic Submission**.
4.  Click **"Start Quiz"**.
    -   The browser will ask to enter **Full-Screen Mode**. You MUST check/allow this to proceed.

### 3. Taking the Quiz (Anti-Cheating Rules)
Once the quiz starts, the following rules apply:
-   **DO NOT switch tabs**: Leaving the quiz tab will trigger a violation warning.
-   **DO NOT exit full-screen**: Pressing `Esc` or minimizing the window is a violation.
-   **DO NOT minimize the browser**: Keep the window open and active.
-   **No Copy/Paste**: Right-click, Copy, Cut, and Paste functions are disabled.

**Consequences**:
-   **1st & 2nd Violation**: You will receive a popup warning. You must acknowledge it to continue.
-   **3rd Violation**: The quiz will **automatically submit** immediately with your current progress. You cannot undo this.

### 4. Submission
-   Select an answer for every question. All questions are mandatory.
-   Click **"Submit Quiz"** when finished.
-   You typically cannot edit answers after submission.
-   Your score and percentage will be displayed immediately.

-   **"Quiz Not Available"**: The quiz might be inactive, expired, or hasn't started yet. Contact your instructor.
-   **"You have already submitted..."**: You cannot retake the quiz with the same email/register number.
